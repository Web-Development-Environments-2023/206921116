About Me

My dear friends, I am Albus Dumbledore. I was born in 1881 to magical parents, Percival and Kendra. Despite the challenges of my early years, I excelled academically and magically, and became a professor of Transfiguration at Hogwarts after my own graduation. I also became heavily involved in the fight against dark magic and served as the head of the Order of the Phoenix during the First Wizarding War. As headmaster of Hogwarts, I dedicated my life to protecting the wizarding world from dark forces and promoting kindness and respect for all magical beings. Though I never married or had children of my own, my passions included reading, studying ancient magic, playing wizard's chess, and indulging in chocolate, especially Chocolate Frogs.
